My Portfolio

Slave I - Stargames

Slave I was the third year university project. The first time I was part of a big project as the team was formed by 18 people. The game is a cooperative space shooter with the look and feel of a Star Wars game but developed in our own 3D engine . I was in charge of the level design, including missions, cut scenes and dialogue.

If I left you wanting more, here is the official webpage of the game:Slave One Web

Zelda Legends -

CatPad Games

Zelda Legends is a local 2vs2 Battle Arena based on (and as a tribute to) The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past. I was in charge of the QA of the game and also as an assistant designer/programmer. Most of the QA job was organizational to minimize future errors, designing contention plans and managing the bug distribution. Developed in own 2D engine (C++)

To know more about this project:ZeldaLegends Web

And my personal contribution:Guillem Dominguez

Unreal Tournament-

CTF map

CTF-Dune is a multiplayer Capture the Flag map done in Unreal Tournament. It was a 2 week project to test multiplayer level design concepts.

The whole project was monitored and reported. Wiki Available:CTF-Dune Wiki

AugmentedKeroro

Augmented Keroro is an AR arcade game for mobile devices. Both players compete to avoid the obstacles, making their avatars jump with vuforia markers. Developed in Unity3D
