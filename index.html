Slave I - Stargames

Slave I was the third year university project. The first time I was part of a big project as the team was formed by 18 people. The game is a cooperative space shooter with the look and feel of a Star Wars game but developed in our own 3D engine.

I was in charge of the level design, including missions, cut scenes and dialogue.

Zelda Legends - CatPad Games

Zelda Legends is a local 2vs2 Battle Arena based on (and as a tribute to) The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past.

I was in charge of the QA of the game and also as an assistant designer/programmer. Most of the QA job was organizational to minimize future errors, designing contention plans and managing the bug distribution.
